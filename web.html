Welcome To Start Right International School

 
Start Right International Children School(SRICS) came into existence through the yearnings of parents and friends of the school who have in one way or the other been touched with the wonderful academic work earlier displayed by Chief J.O. Sanusi (The proprietor of the school) in the primary cadre.

In Nigeria, there is a strong emphasis on formal school attendance for the necessity of obtaining a certificate. However, Start Right International Children School(SRICS) stresses the need to place her students on education and what is learned in school rather than certificate and entry into jobs.
